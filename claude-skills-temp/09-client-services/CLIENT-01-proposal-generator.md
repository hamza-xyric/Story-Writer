## Purpose

Creates client-centric proposals that lead with business problems, not Xyric services. Proposals are built around solving Data Problems and AI Problems through a phased partnership journey, positioning Xyric as a long-term partner rather than a one-time vendor.

**Core Principle**: The client's pain is the hero. Xyric is the guide.

**Triggers**:
- "Create a proposal for [client]"
- "Draft a proposal for [project]"

**Prerequisites**:
- Discovery information gathered (use PRE-01 Pre-Proposal Discovery skill if needed)

## Client Problem Bundles

### Bundle 1: Data Problems
**Client Experience**: "We're drowning in spreadsheets, decisions take forever, no single source of truth"

| Pain Point | What They Say | Business Impact |
|------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Scattered Data | "Our data is everywhere - 15 different spreadsheets, 3 databases, 5 SaaS tools" | Hours wasted finding information, errors from copy-paste |
| Manual Reporting | "Someone spends 2 days every week compiling reports" | $50K+/year in analyst time, always outdated |
| Slow Decisions | "By the time I get the numbers, the opportunity is gone" | Missed revenue, reactive instead of proactive |
| No Confidence | "I don't trust these numbers - they never match" | Decisions based on gut, not data |

**Typical Outcomes**:
- 10-40 hours/week reclaimed from manual data work
- Real-time dashboards replacing weekly Excel reports
- 80-95% reduction in data errors
- 3-6 month ROI from time savings and better decisions

---

### Bundle 2: AI Problems
**Client Experience**: "We can't scale, too many manual processes, team overwhelmed with repetitive work"

| Pain Point | What They Say | Business Impact |
|------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Support Overload | "We're buried in the same 20 questions asked 100 different ways" | $15-25/ticket cost, slow response times, burnout |
| Knowledge Silos | "Only Sarah knows how to do that, and she's on vacation" | Single points of failure, onboarding takes months |
| Manual Processes | "We're copying data between 5 systems every day" | Human errors, can't scale, staff frustration |
| Repetitive Tasks | "My team is doing the same thing over and over" | Low morale, high turnover, can't focus on high-value work |

**Typical Outcomes**:
- 40-60% reduction in support costs
- 24/7 availability with intelligent automation
- 70-85% of routine inquiries handled without humans
- Staff freed for strategic, high-value work

## Anti-Patterns

### âŒ Service-Centric Behaviors (NEVER DO)
- Listing service codes (DATA-1.1, AI-2.3) in proposals
- Leading with "We offer..." instead of "You're facing..."
- Presenting a menu of services to choose from
- Focusing on features instead of outcomes
- Using technical jargon the client doesn't use

### âŒ Vendor Behaviors (NEVER DO)
- Positioning as one-time project vendor
- Hourly billing as default
- "Build and disappear" language
- No mention of ongoing partnership

### âŒ Generic Behaviors (NEVER DO)
- Sending template without heavy customization
- Not referencing specific client pain points from discovery
- Using generic industry examples instead of their situation
- Skipping the ASSESS phase (always understand before proposing)
