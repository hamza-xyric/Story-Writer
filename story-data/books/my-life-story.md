# My Life Story

## Overview

This book captures my journey from childhood to the present day. It follows a **hybrid structure**: chronological life phases provide the backbone, while thematic threads weave through time to connect related memories.

The book is being written piece by piece - not in order, but following whatever memories call to me. The system helps organize fragments into a coherent narrative, learning my voice along the way.
